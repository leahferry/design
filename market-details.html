Revival Market

Revival Market reimagines the historic Navy Yard Car Barn as a layered, multisensory food hall where architecture and cuisine converge to tell stories of transit, memory, and cultural revival. Rooted in the concepts of layering and stacking, the design transforms the building into a living palimpsest, with shifting levels, overlapping surfaces, and stacked programs that encourage exploration and echo the siteâ€™s history of movement. Revived recipes from historic streetcar cities connect visitors to shared urban histories, while flexible stalls and pop-up spaces support local vendors and cultivate evolving community experiences. More than a nod to the past, the space emphasizes interaction, rediscovery, and cultural flow, creating a gathering place shaped by memory, movement, and the people who continue to animate it.
