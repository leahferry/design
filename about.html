About Me

Hi, I’m Leah Ferry, an interior design student with a deep passion for creating spaces that connect people to nature, experience, and one another. My love for the outdoors, plants, and the organic beauty of curves heavily influences my work, as does my interest in immersive, experiential environments that engage the senses and invite people to slow down and connect.

I approach design as an opportunity to craft spaces that evoke emotion, promote balance, and support well-being, especially within community-centered settings. I’m drawn to the calming greens of nature and the textures of the earth, weaving them into layered environments that feel alive, nurturing, and meaningful. Whether through biophilic strategies or spatial storytelling, I aim to design places that leave a lasting impression and foster belonging.

When I’m not designing, you’ll find me reading, hiking, or exploring the outdoors, experiences that continually inspire my approach to thoughtful, people-focused interiors.
